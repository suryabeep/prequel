About Us

Damien Peyre

Damien Peyre is the French speaking and GCC countries BD. He has been working with Retail since 2005 after a first career part in various industries. Now focusing on digital DtoC like marketplaces for start-up’s and DNVB’s.

He worked in food, fashion, lifestyle, beauty, healthcare, CPG… as CMO at Carré Blanc in charge of brand transformation and digital marketing, Global Merchandising Director at Pierre Fabre and Director of Category Procurement at Carrefour.

He was instrumental in the settlement of retail operations in China then Carrefour store expansion in India ; he also internaly advised Carrefour executives on commercial digital transformation and customer experience.

Besides he advised retail companies such as Al Meera Qatar, Marjane/Acima Morocco, Gifi France, Metro Ukraine on their omnichannel strategies.

Damien created a number of products during his early stage in design at SEB, Renault... He also owns intellectual properties in the areas of machine and interface design.

He did in France his MBA at ESLSCA and got specializations in the fields of UX design at ENSAAMA, of e-commerce & digital marketing at ILV and of international business at INSEAD.
